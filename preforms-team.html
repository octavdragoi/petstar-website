Preforms Division

About Our Preforms Division

Our Preforms Division represents the cutting edge of PET preform manufacturing technology. With state-of-the-art injection molding equipment and rigorous quality control processes, we produce millions of high-quality preforms annually for the beverage and packaging industries.

We specialize in custom preform solutions, working closely with our clients to develop preforms that meet their exact specifications for weight, neck finish, color, and performance characteristics.
