Recycling Division

Leading the way in sustainable bottle-to-bottle PET recycling

About Our Recycling Division

Our Recycling Division operates state-of-the-art bottle-to-bottle recycling facilities, transforming post-consumer PET waste into premium food-grade recycled PET (rPET) material. We are at the forefront of the circular economy, closing the loop between waste collection and new product manufacturing.

With EFSA and FDA approvals, our rPET meets the highest international standards for food contact applications, making it suitable for use in beverage bottles and food packaging.

Our Process

Bottle-to-Bottle Technology

A comprehensive multi-stage process ensuring the highest quality rPET

01

Collection & Sorting

PET bottles are collected and sorted using advanced optical sorting technology to separate by color and type

02

Grinding & Washing

Bottles are ground into flakes and undergo multi-stage washing to remove labels, adhesives, and contaminants

03

Decontamination

Advanced thermal and chemical decontamination processes ensure food-grade safety standards are met

04

Extrusion & Pelletizing

Clean flakes are melted, filtered, and extruded into high-quality rPET pellets ready for manufacturing

05

Quality Testing

Rigorous laboratory testing ensures all rPET meets food-grade specifications and regulatory requirements
